LEARN MORE ABOUT US...

Outech secure enjoy long standing partnerships with some of the world’s biggest tracking device manufacturers and these partnerships have seen us pioneering innovational device features and tracking practices. Because we have proven our proficiency over the years, we have become an important port of call for crucial development consultations. For some of them our impressive showing has earned us the trust to become the sole representatives of their brands here in Nigeria. Locally, we are among the first tracking companies to get tracking license from the Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC) and today we are a sort of reference standard for upcoming tracking companies. Our high-priority partnerships with the Nigerian Police Force and other Nigerian security agencies means that we are always exposed to the most formidable security network and the largest array of measures.

Vehicle tracking is a technology-based service and because we and our partners understand that technology is extremely dynamic, we are constantly innovating our products and processes to enable us deliver state-of -the-art tracking solutions. Owing to this we have seen ourselves progress from the times of purely SMS tracking to today’s real time GPRS server tracking and we are not slowing down. We deploy unconventional combination of technology to ensure our clients get nothing less than the best services.

The Outech secure Control Centre is the component that ties all of the above neatly together. A vibrant workforce, state-of-the-art equipment and clever procedures all converge to ensure that we always have any situation under control. Because we understand that things can go wrong (or sometimes, out of control) in a split second, our processes and efforts are always proactive and precautionary in nature enabling us to always stay steps ahead of the bad guys.
